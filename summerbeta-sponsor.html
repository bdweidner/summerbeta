// Sponsorship Opportunities — 2026
SUMMER BETA

Summer Beta is a free, independent game development competition for local high school students, Rowan University students, and Rowan alumni. Organized by Benjamin Weidner, Assistant Teaching Professor at Rowan University.

We are raising a student prize fund for our inaugural event. 100% of prize fund contributions go directly to the winning developers.

// Prize Fund Contributions
